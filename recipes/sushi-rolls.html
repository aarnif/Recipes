Garden Delight Sushi Roll

Ingredients

Sushi Rice

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1¼ cups water
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Filling

  • 2 medium-sized carrots, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 4-5 radishes, thinly sliced
  • ½ red bell pepper, julienned
  • Fresh spinach leaves or microgreens

Protein

  • 4 oz. smoked salmon or cooked shrimp (optional)
  • OR Tofu strips (for a vegetarian option)

Wrapping and Topping

  • 4 nori sheets
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for serving

Special Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sushi rice by rinsing, cooking, and seasoning with vinegar mixture. Spread on a plate to cool.
  2. Julienne and slice the filling ingredients. Set aside.
  3. Combine the ingredients for the special sauce in a bowl. Adjust to taste.
  4. Assemble the sushi rolls by spreading rice on a nori sheet, adding fillings, and rolling tightly with a bamboo mat.
  5. Cut the rolls into pieces, garnish with sesame seeds, and serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
